What are Awe Walks?

An “awe walk” is a stroll in which you intentionally shift your attention outward instead of inward.

In Fall 2020, Neuroscientist Dr. Virginia Sturm lead a scientific study that showed that going on 15 minute walks looking for things that inspire awe can promote brain health in older adults.
